My Journey as a Librarian

I was born in Afghanistan in 1982, but due to the war with the Soviet Union, my family sought refuge in Iran when I was just two years old. I completed my education in Tehran and later pursued a degree in Information Science and Librarianship at Al-Zahra University, graduating in 2008.

Professional Experience

  • Worked in university and public libraries after graduation.
  • Served as a librarian at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Library in Kabul, Afghanistan.
  • Implemented the Library of Congress classification system in the library.
  • Moved to the United States in 2016 to continue my career in librarianship.

Skills & Expertise

  1. Library of Congress Classification (LCC)
  2. Cataloging and Metadata Management
  3. University and Public Library Services
  4. Information Science Research
